Wildlandfire.thairen.net.th is a collaboration among Royal Forest Department, Office of Information Technology Administration for Educational Development (UniNet), Upper ASEAN Wildland Fire Special Research Unit, Forestry Research Center, Faculty of Forestry, Kasetsart University and Webster University Thailand. All fire emissions data are from Copernicus Atmosphere Monitoring Service (CAMS) Global Fire Assimilation System (GFAS).

FAQ

Q: What is the purpose of this site?
A: The purpose is to educate the public and provide tools and data visualization demonstrations for students.
Q: Where does the data come from and why is it one day late?
A: The data comes from ECMWF, the European Centre for Medium-Range Weather Forecasts. Its origin is the European Copernicus satellite. Due to satellite data transfer and initial processing of their data at ECMWF, there are 1-2 day delays.
Q: How are the maps and graphs built?
A: We use R scripts, please see Github.
